Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

What we collect

When you sign in or RSVP, we store your name, phone number, email address, and attendance status in a private Google Sheet.

Authentication

We use Firebase Authentication (Google, Facebook, and phone/SMS) to verify your identity. Firebase manages your credentials; we never see your passwords. See Firebase's privacy policy.

How we use it

Your information is used solely for wedding planning — sending updates, tracking RSVPs, and coordinating with vendors. We do not share, sell, or use your data for any other purpose.

Local storage

We use browser session storage to manage sign-in state. No tracking scripts or analytics are used.

Contact

To request access to, correction of, or deletion of your data, email the couple directly.
